## The structure of providers in Coremail	[Back](./../coremail.md)

Providers means the running and managing part of a whole Coremail system, which should be single. In a provider, there will be multiple organizations, supporting multiple domains. For users in such a system, Coremail has provided different levels of servers (COS), which is enumenable to maintain resources.
